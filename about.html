About Us

Nkore Drum is dedicated to bringing the untold stories from Western Uganda to light. Our mission is to celebrate the rich heritage, culture, and everyday stories of the region while inspiring growth and awareness.

Our Mission

To amplify voices from Western Uganda and beyond, creating a platform for storytelling, cultural preservation, and social impact.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for authentic stories, connecting communities and fostering growth through knowledge and understanding.
